Vehicle Scrapping Specialist

China's vehicle scrapping market has been characterized by irregularities and cumbersome processes. The purpose of this project is to provide car owners with a legal and efficient way to scrap their cars, and to give them emotional care in the process. The project consists of a TO C app and a TO B app, the TO C app is for car owners to facilitate the scrapping process and also serves as a platform for the secondary sale of recyclable accessories from scrapped cars, while the TO B app is for employees to operate accordingly. In addition, a keepsake was designed to provide emotional care to the users.
