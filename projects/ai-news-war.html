AI News War

In the future, with the development of AI technology, while people enjoy the convenience of life brought by AI, they will also face the hazards of abusing AI. This project envisions the impact of AI on the political sphere in the future of journalism, and designs a VR game to simulate the AI news war scenario, so that players can immerse themselves in contemplating the ethical issues of generative artificial intelligence.
