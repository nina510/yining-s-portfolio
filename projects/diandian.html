DIANDIAN: Mobile Charging System

DIANDIAN is a mobile charging system, which contains mobile charging post, vehicle HMI and Vehicle-Road-Cloud Platform, designed to solve the problem of charging difficulty on highways during peak holiday periods in China, providing car owners with high-speed, intelligent and considerate charging solution.

Background

▶ Holiday travel dilemma in China

➤ EVs take more than twice time to travel to their destinations due to difficulties of charging in service-areas on highways during holidays.

▶ Service-areas on Chinese Highways

➤ About 6,000 service-areas in China
➤ Average of 4.2 charging piles per service-area
➤ Each EV queues for 2hr+ to be charged at peak times

▶ Retired EV batteries in China

➤ Batteries retire when the capacity is less than 80%, but they can still be used in other scenarios
➤ There are already 580k+ retired batteries until 2023
➤ The recycling market for retired batteries is expected to exceed 100 billion yuan by 2030

▶ VOLVO's battery recycling planning

➤ Volvo plans to reuse retired batteries in energy storage systems
➤ Aiming to achieve carbon neutrality by 2040
➤ Exploring innovative solutions for sustainable development
